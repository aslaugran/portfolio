Flikin' Fluke - betri fyrirsögn!

Who doesn't relate to sitting on the sofa on a stormy night with your significent other and neither of your can deside on a film to watch. That's what Flickin' Fluke was created to solve. It's a web app that helps lost souls finding equally lost films. The idea was inspired by websites like The Fucking Weather and and the project is done in collaboration with Jón Tryggvi

My part in the project included both design and development, we used angular for building the web app and two seperate API's to flick through films and get trailers. Developing the idea, designing it and developing was done in mere 3 weeks. Below you can see a few frames from the project but nothing beats the real stuff. Flickin' Fluke is up and running, check it out and while your at it, check out Jón Tryggvi's online portfolio also.

Go Poop

This project was inspired by a poster we stumbled upon online on day. It illustrates human poop, both healthy and unhealthy. This resourse was bound to have us laughing throughout the project so we desided to computerize it as a poop test and poop diary for everyone's better helth.

The poop test had two catigories, texture and color and after you entered your "poop of the day" you got your resaults with a bit of healthy advise attached to it. In the design prosess we thought it was important to keep the illustrations very cute and happy, unlike most peoples stool.

You could also log in to your own personal poop diary, called Dirarya, and collect your test resaults over a period of time and then compare your shit with other peoples shit or see how you get on with your stool with time. The programming was done in php and we stored the data in sql database.

There were poop stories, which you and others could comment on and share, good advice and more was about to come. This project isn't online yet, but will hopefully soon claim it's place on the wold wide web. This was done in collabiration with Jón Tryggvi and Herdís
